What is the COCP?

The problem is not the people.
It is the organisation.

The COCP teaches practitioners how to identify and address the conditions that create the Excellence Tax™: the extra labour, identity suppression, and personal cost that Black professionals carry in corporate workplaces.

Most diversity programmes focus on changing the individual. The evidence shows this does not work. The COCP is built on a different position: the organisation must change, and practitioners need the tools and the method to make that happen.

Who is it for?

For professionals working in HR, DEI, and change management equally.

Candidates need at least five years of work experience, including two or more years in change, HR, DEI, or consulting.

HR Directors and People Leaders

Those who lead on culture and inclusion strategy and want a research-based method to drive real change.

DEI Professionals

Those ready to move beyond awareness training into work that focuses on the organisation and how it needs to change.

Change Management Professionals

Consultants and practitioners looking to add a culturally intelligent method to their practice.

Organisational Development

Those expanding their capability to work with diverse workforce contexts.

Black Professionals Moving into Consulting

Those with change or HR experience who want to build a consulting practice using lived and professional expertise.

Programme Structure

Seven modules. One clear method.

01
Change Management Theory and Cultural Intelligence

Where current frameworks fail and how to position the Excellence Tax methodology within change management practice.

02
The Excellence Tax Diagnostic

The Four-Quadrant Model, the Excellence Tax Scorecard, root cause analysis, and the cost of inaction.

03
Intervention Design and Change Strategy

Designing change strategies, stakeholder plans, communication, and success metrics.

04
Implementation and Delivery

Running pilot programmes, scaling change across organisations, and building leadership capability.

05
Resistance Management and Political Navigation

Managing four types of resistance and holding difficult conversations without losing integrity.

06
Measurement, Sustainability, and Embedding

Measuring impact and building long-term change into organisational systems.

07
Consulting Practice and Business Development

Pricing services, writing proposals, and building a sustainable consulting practice.

Why this is different

Culture change, not compliance training.

Grounded in original research

Every module is built on data from 1,039 Black professionals across four countries. This is not theoretical. It is evidence-based practice.

Focused on the organisation

The qualification does not ask Black professionals to adapt further. It equips practitioners to change the systems and cultures that extract from them.

A diagnostic tool included

Practitioners receive a licence to use the Excellence Tax Diagnostic with individuals and within organisations, with no additional fee.

Built for scale

The qualification is designed to certify practitioners who can take the methodology into organisations, not create dependency on a single consultant.

Study Options

Both formats lead to the COCP post-nominal designation and entry into the certified practitioner directory.
