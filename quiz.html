The Excellence Tax™ Framework

Our research identified fifteen specific burdens extracted from Black professionals, organised into five core categories. These taxes compound rather than diminish with career advancement, contradicting expectations that success brings security.

5

Resistance Taxes

Evidence Stewardship | Infrastructure Building | Intergenerational Load

The unpaid "justice labour" performed to protect those who follow, encompassing evidence stewardship and intergenerational load.

4

Leadership Taxes

Threshold Fatigue | Unresourced Sponsorship | Exposure without Protection

Challenges that paradoxically intensify with seniority, such as threshold fatigue and exposure without protection.

3

Systemic Taxes

Bias Navigation | Microaggression Absorption | Representational Burden

These reflect institutional design and include bias navigation, microaggression absorption, and representational burden.

2

Survival Taxes

Code Switching | Voice Suppression | Emotional Regulation

These daily adaptations govern self-presentation, including code-switching, voice suppression, and emotional regulation.

1

Foundation Taxes

Performance Burden | Proof Burden | Perfection Burden

These are the entry-level levies required just to establish baseline professional credibility: the performance, proof, and perfection burdens.
