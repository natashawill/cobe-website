The Voice Behind the Data

Natasha Williams

Lead Researcher, Wellbeing Strategist & Somatic Expert

Natasha does not deliver generic corporate motivation. She delivers systemic intervention. Combining 14+ years of executive leadership as a Director of Building Surveying with deep expertise in organisational psychology, Natasha speaks the language of the boardroom.

Having personally survived a career-induced burnout that required hospitalisation, Natasha spent over a year recovering in Jamaica, where she qualified as a trauma-informed somatic resilience coach.

Today, as the founder of The Cost of Black Excellence Research Institute, she synthesises the in-depth data of 98 UK Black professionals with profound somatic intelligence, proving to corporate leaders that employee exhaustion is not a personal failure, but a structural extraction.

Signature Keynotes

Each presentation can be tailored to fit 45-minute summit keynotes, 90-minute executive workshops, or half-day board briefings.

The Excellence Tax: Quantifying Systemic Extraction

The Institute’s flagship presentation. Drawing directly from our proprietary dataset of UK professionals, Natasha introduces the Excellence Tax™ framework. She reveals the hidden £11,170 annual cost per employee and demonstrates exactly how the pressure to overperform drains both human capital and corporate ROI.

Ideal for: C-Suite, HR Summits

The Somatic Toll of Code-Switching

We cannot intellect our way out of burnout. Combining organisational psychology with trauma-informed somatic coaching, Natasha explains how hypervigilance and identity suppression are stored physically in the body, leading to the collapse of diverse talent long before they hand in their resignation.

Ideal for: Wellbeing Conf., ERGs

From Performative to Structural: A New Paradigm

A highly strategic, action-oriented briefing. Natasha challenges existing, superficial D&I initiatives and outlines the "Liberation Infrastructure" needed to create genuine psychological safety. Leaders walk away with actionable frameworks to stop the extraction of their top BAME talent.

Ideal for: Leadership Away-Days
